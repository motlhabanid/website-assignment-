About Us

Welcome to Custom Cubans — your ultimate destination for premium silver and diamond Cuban link jewelry. Founded by Derick Motlhabani, we are passionate about delivering quality, style, and culture through every handcrafted piece.

Our Mission

To provide luxurious, handcrafted jewelry that empowers individuality and celebrates African excellence. We partner with trusted diamond and silver sources like Debswana, Marikana, and Limpopo artisans to bring you the finest pieces.

Why Choose Us?

  • ✅ Ethically sourced materials
  • ✅ Expert craftsmanship
  • ✅ Fast & secure shipping
  • ✅ Trusted by over 10,000 satisfied customers

Meet the Founder

Derick Motlhabani is a visionary entrepreneur driven by a mission to showcase African craftsmanship through modern luxury. With deep roots in cultural identity and creative design, Derick has transformed Custom Cubans into a trusted, recognizable name in high-end jewelry.
